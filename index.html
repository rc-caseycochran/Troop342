Our Philosophy

Scouts Lead. Adults Support.

We believe in the "Youth-led, Adult-sponsored" philosophy. The Scouts run the meetings, plan the outings, and set the calendar. Adults make sure the program is delivered safely and well.

🏔

Outdoor Skills First

Our program centers on real outdoor competency: navigation, camping, cooking, Leave No Trace, and the skills that build confidence in the backcountry and beyond.

🌟

Scouts Teach Scouts

Older Scouts mentor younger ones. Leadership isn't a title here, it's a habit built through teaching, guiding, and taking responsibility for those who come after you.

🎯

Advancement at Your Pace

We strongly encourage advancement, but we never force it. Scouts set their own goals. Many great Scouts never became Eagle Scouts, and that's more than okay.

🤝

Give Back

We push Scouts to volunteer and staff council events: Klondike, Webelos Weekend, NYLT, and Camporee. Service isn't a checkbox, it's part of who we are.
